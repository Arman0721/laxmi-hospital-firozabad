Dr. Lata Rajput

Senior Obstetrician & Gynecologist

Dr. Lata Rajput is Firozabad's premier female gynecologist and fertility expert. With over 12 years of specialized clinical practice, she is renowned for her medical precision in supervising high-risk pregnancies, performing minimal access laparoscopic keyhole surgeries, and managing painless labor deliveries.

Clinical Expertise

Under her direction, Laxmi Hospital operates highly sterile labor suites connected directly to a Level-3 NICU. Her surgical and clinical focus areas include:

  • Management of maternal gestational high blood pressure (Pre-Eclampsia) and gestational diabetes.
  • Advanced epidural analgesia coordinates for painless, high-comfort deliveries.
  • Minimal access laparoscopic removals of fibroids, ovarian cysts, and clinical hysterectomies.
  • Follicular monitoring, infertility assessments, and hormone therapy calibrations.

Education & Fellowships

  • M.B.B.S: Renowned Government Medical College, India.
  • M.S. (Obstetrics & Gynecology): Highly Accredited Post-Graduate Medical Institute.
  • D.G.O (Diploma in Gynecology & Obstetrics): Leading State University.
  • Fellowship: Advanced Gynecological Endoscopy & Laparoscopy.

Academic Affiliations

Active Fellow of the Federation of Obstetric and Gynecological Societies of India (FOGSI) and the Indian Society of Assisted Reproduction (ISAR).
