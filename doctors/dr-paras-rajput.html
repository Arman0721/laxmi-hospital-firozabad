Dr. Paras Rajput

Senior Consultant Pediatrician

Dr. Paras Rajput is Firozabad's premier child health specialist and neonatal critical intensivist. With over 10 years of dedicated clinical practice, he is highly trusted for his precision in managing premature newborn stabilization, structuring immunization programs, and resolving acute pediatric pulmonary conditions.

Clinical Expertise

Under his guidance, Laxmi Hospital operates a level-3 Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) with dedicated incubators. His clinical focus areas include:

  • Immediate stabilizing care for premature, very low-birth-weight, and septic newborns.
  • Formulation and execution of complete pediatric immunizations under WHO cold-chain standards.
  • Advanced pediatric allergy testing, respiratory therapies, and nebulizer setups.
  • Regular childhood growth mapping, nutrition panels, and early developmental evaluations.

Education & Training

  • M.B.B.S: Elite Government Medical University, India.
  • M.D. (Pediatric Medicine): Renowned Post-Graduate Medical Institute.
  • D.C.H (Diploma in Child Health): Leading Pediatric Clinical Institute.
  • Fellowship: Advanced Neonatology Intensive Care Support.

Professional Accreditations

Active Life Fellow of the Indian Academy of Pediatrics (IAP) and the National Neonatology Forum of India (NNF).

Laxmi Multi-Speciality Hospital is Firozabad's premier destination for critical trauma relief, orthopedic surgery, neonatal care, and professional outpatient consultations.
