Dr. Mohammad Shafiq

Consultant Orthopedic & Trauma Surgeon

Dr. Mohammad Shafiq is an experienced Orthopedic and Trauma Specialist dedicated to providing advanced care for bone, joint, spine, and trauma-related conditions. He specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of fractures, joint replacement surgeries, spine disorders, sports injuries, and complex trauma cases.

With a patient-centered approach and the use of modern medical techniques, Dr. Shafiq focuses on delivering accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and faster recovery for his patients. His expertise includes managing neck and back pain, arthritis, spinal conditions, accident-related injuries, and orthopedic surgical procedures.

"Committed to restoring mobility, relieving pain, and improving quality of life."

Clinical Expertise

Dr. Shafiq performs advanced musculoskeletal interventions at Laxmi Hospital. His core clinical areas of expertise include:

  • Fracture & Trauma Management: Reconstructive surgical alignment of complex trauma and emergency accident injuries.
  • Joint Replacement Surgery: Total hip and knee replacements using high-performance wear-resistant implants.
  • Spine Surgery & Spine Disorders: Surgical and non-surgical management of disc herniations, sciatica, and spinal stenosis.
  • Sports Injury Treatment: Keyhole arthroscopic repair of ligaments (ACL/PCL), meniscus, and muscle tears.
  • Arthritis & Pain Care: Advanced medication regimens, intra-articular injections, and joint preservation therapies.

Education & Training

  • M.B.B.S: Recognized medical institution, India.
  • M.S. (Orthopedics): Accredited post-graduate medical institution, India.
  • Trauma Life Support Training: Certified in emergency trauma life saving procedures.
