Dr. Amit Gupta

Senior Consultant Orthopedic Surgeon

Dr. Amit Gupta is Firozabad's leading specialist in joint replacement arthroplasties and complex orthopedic skeletal trauma. With over 15 years of surgical leadership, he is highly recognized for performing knee/hip reconstructions, arthroscopic keyhole ligament repairs, and advanced spine stabilizations.

Clinical Expertise

Under his surgical guidance, Laxmi Hospital operates modular surgical theatres with 100% bacterial control barriers. His clinical specialties include:

  • Total Knee and Hip Replacement surgeries using high-durability modern implants.
  • Reconstruction and open reductions of severe highway polytrauma bone fractures.
  • Minimal access arthroscopic repairs of ACL, PCL, meniscus, and shoulder labrums.
  • Decompressions, disc corrections, and spinal alignments for chronic back conditions.

Education & Training

  • M.B.B.S: Top-tier State Government Medical College, India.
  • M.S. (Orthopedic Surgery): Elite Medical University, India.
  • Fellowship: Joint Replacement Arthroplasty & Arthroscopic Reconstruction.
  • Surgical Residency: Advanced Trauma Care & Bone Stabilization.

Professional Accreditations

Active Life Member of the Indian Orthopaedic Association (IOA), UP Orthopaedic Association (UPOA), and the Indian Society of Hip and Knee Surgeons (ISHKS).

Laxmi Multi-Speciality Hospital is Firozabad's premier destination for critical trauma relief, orthopedic surgery, neonatal care, and professional outpatient consultations.
