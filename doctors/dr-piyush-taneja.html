Dr. Piyush Taneja

Consultant Physician

Dr. Piyush Taneja is a highly experienced Consultant Physician (M.B.B.S., M.D. Medicine) dedicated to providing comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of medical conditions. With a patient-centric approach and evidence-based treatment methods, Dr. Taneja focuses on delivering accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and long-term health management for every patient.

He specializes in managing diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disorders, respiratory diseases, infectious diseases, and lifestyle-related health concerns. Under his guidance, the internal medicine clinic at Laxmi Hospital provides clinical screenings, diagnostic triage, and personalized health management schedules.

"Dedicated to accurate diagnostic investigations, compassionate patient care, and sustainable health management."

Clinical Expertise

Dr. Taneja leads critical medical diagnostics and therapeutic regimes at Laxmi Hospital. His core clinical areas include:

  • Diabetes Mellitus: Tailored glycemic profiling, custom insulin adjustments, and metabolic lifestyle counseling.
  • Hypertension Management: Regulating cardiovascular arterial pressures and diagnostic screening for target-organ safety.
  • Thyroid Disorders: Management of hypo/hyperthyroidism, goiters, and metabolic hormonal imbalances.
  • Respiratory Care: Diagnostic screening and clinical control regimes for asthma, seasonal COPD flares, and allergies.
  • Infectious Diseases: Triage and clinical management of vector-borne illnesses (Dengue, Malaria, Typhoid) and acute fevers.
  • Lifestyle Health: Management plans for chronic cholesterol imbalances, high lipids, obesity, and stress-induced health concerns.

Education & Fellowships

  • M.B.B.S: Accredited Government Medical College, India.
  • M.D. (Internal Medicine): Highly recognized post-graduate medical institution, India.
  • Clinical Fellowships: Specialized training courses in Advanced Diabetes and Lifestyle Medicine.

Academic Memberships

Active life member of the Association of Physicians of India (API) and the Indian Medical Association (IMA).
