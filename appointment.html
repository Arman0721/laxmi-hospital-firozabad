OPD Booking FAQs

Got questions? Learn how our consultation token systems and checkups operate.

What happens after I submit this online request?

Our dynamic appointment system generates a digital token receipt instantly and caches it. A clinical representative will send a confirmation SMS detailing your exact token number and suggested check-in window to eliminate long lobby delays.

Can I reschedule or cancel my booking token?

Yes. Simply click the "Confirm Arrival on WhatsApp" link generated upon booking completion or text our desk at +91 7078221122 with your Booking ID to reschedule free of charge.

Do you accept insurance or TPA cashless facilities?

Yes. Laxmi Hospital accepts cashless treatments across all major government and private TPAs (such as Star Health, HDFC Ergo, ICICI Lombard, Ayushman Bharat, etc.) for both planned surgeries and acute trauma cases.
