General & Appointments

What are the OPD timings at Laxmi Hospital?

Our Outpatient Department (OPD) operates from Monday to Saturday, between 9:00 AM and 7:00 PM. Emergency services and critical care units are open 24/7, 365 days a year.

How can I book an appointment with a specialist?

You can book an appointment online via our Appointment Booking Page, chat with us on WhatsApp, or call our OPD Hotline at +91 7078221122 or 05612-297700 for instant scheduling.

Do I need a referral to see a doctor at Laxmi Hospital?

No, a prior referral is not mandatory. You can walk in or schedule a consultation directly with any of our specialists. However, if you are seeking coverage under certain government schemes or specific insurance policies, a referral from a primary medical officer might be required.

Insurance & Cashless TPA

Does Laxmi Hospital offer cashless treatment?

Yes. We have cashless hospitalization agreements with all major private health insurance providers and Third Party Administrators (TPAs). Please visit our billing desk upon admission with your valid insurance card and e-card details for smooth verification.

Is the Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY scheme accepted?

Yes, Laxmi Multi-Speciality Hospital is fully empanelled under the Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY government scheme. Eligible cardholders can receive cashless treatment for approved surgical and medical procedures under this protocol.

Emergency & Facilities

What emergency services are available?

We provide 24/7 emergency response, including a dedicated Level-1 Trauma Care center, Level-3 ICU beds, modular operating theatres, and advanced life support (ALS) ambulances equipped with ventilators and defibrillators to stabilize critical patients on the way.

What is your visitor policy for admitted patients?

To preserve a sterile recovery atmosphere, only one attendant with a valid pass is allowed to stay with the patient. General visiting hours are from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM daily. Visitors are requested to sanitize their hands and wear masks inside critical wards.

Laxmi Multi-Speciality Hospital is Firozabad's premier destination for critical trauma relief, orthopedic surgery, neonatal care, and professional outpatient consultations.
