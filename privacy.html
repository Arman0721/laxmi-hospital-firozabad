Privacy Policy

Home  /  Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 01, 2026

Laxmi Multi-Speciality Hospital, Firozabad is committed to protecting the privacy, security, and confidentiality of our patients, website visitors, and digital users. This Privacy Policy details how we compile, process, and secure personal information gathered through our online OPD scheduler, contact inquiries, and live help chatbot.

1. Types of Personal Information We Collect

When you interact with our online services, we may collect the following parameters:

  • Patient Contact Information: Name, mobile number, email address, and home location when scheduling OPD consultations or submitting inquiries.
  • Clinical Details: Preferred department, symptoms checklist, or consulting doctor specifications requested during registration.
  • Technical Metadata: IP address, device browser configuration, and page interaction timestamps gathered to optimize responsive formatting and secure server protocols.

2. How We Utilize Your Data

We process your personal information solely to provide top-tier clinical operations, specifically:

  • To review and confirm your online OPD appointment registrations.
  • To dispatch emergency support or ambulances when requested through our dispatches hotline.
  • To verify patient credentials and maintain accurate internal clinic logs.
  • To address medical questions or follow-up feedback sent through our contact forms.

3. Patients Clinical Records and Confidentiality

At Laxmi Hospital, all patient case profiles, treatment diagnoses, prescription histories, and laboratory records are handled under strict medical confidentiality protocols. In compliance with the Indian Medical Council (Professional Conduct, Etiquette and Ethics) Regulations, we do not disclose patient clinical parameters to any external third party, except under the following conditions:

  • When explicitly authorized by the patient or their legal guardian for insurance claims or referrals.
  • When mandated by statutory public health authorities or legal court warrants.

4. No Sharing or Sale of Personal Data

We never sell, rent, trade, or distribute your email addresses, phone numbers, or profile information to commercial marketing bureaus or advertiser lists. Access to your contact records is restricted to authorized hospital administrative desks and medical staff who need the details to arrange your treatments.

5. Secure Storage and Data Retention

We implement appropriate physical, technical, and administrative security measures to protect your patient records against loss, misuse, or unauthorized access. Your online data remains active in our database systems only as long as necessary to fulfill clinical follow-ups or satisfy statutory document retention laws.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website utilizes minor server cookies to save user preferences, such as keeping local admin dashboard credentials saved or recording review updates. You can configure your browser to reject cookies, but this might restrict certain interactive components (like local dashboard storage) from operating fully.

7. Revisions to This Policy

Laxmi Hospital reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We encourage users to check this page periodically to remain informed about how we are securing your information.
