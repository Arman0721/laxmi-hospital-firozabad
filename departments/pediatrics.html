Child & Neonate Care

Advanced Pediatrics & Specialized Level-3 NICU Support

The Pediatrics department at Laxmi Hospital Firozabad is a highly calibrated center dedicated to newborn healthcare, growth assessments, and critical neonatal emergencies. Led by child specialist Dr. Paras Rajput, we provide compassionate and evidence-based treatments for infants, toddlers, and young adolescents.

We are equipped with Firozabad's most advanced level-3 Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) featuring high-end incubators, double-surface phototherapy sets, and low-flow respiratory oxygen support to safeguard premature births and low-birth-weight babies.

Core Clinical Treatments Offered

  • Mandatory WHO Immunizations: Complete vaccination programs from birth to 15 years with detailed monitoring charts.
  • Neonatal ICU (NICU) Care: Continuous cardiorespiratory support, warmth maintenance, and tube feeding for premature newborns.
  • Pediatric Asthma & Allergy Care: Advanced nebulization pathways, pulmonary function screens, and allergen containment protocols.
  • Developmental Milestone Reviews: Growth tracking, nutritional guidelines, and early identification of developmental delays.

Pediatrics FAQs

At what weight does a newborn baby require NICU admission?

Generally, newborns weighing less than 1.8 kg (low birth weight) or born before 34 weeks of gestation require NICU admission at Laxmi Hospital to help them regulate body temperatures, stabilize breathing patterns, and receive secure enteral tube feeds.

Are childhood vaccinations available on all days?

Yes. Laxmi Hospital's immunization room operates everyday from 10:00 AM to 02:00 PM. We store both painless and standard mandatory vaccines under exact medical cold-chain refrigeration levels to ensure peak efficacy.
