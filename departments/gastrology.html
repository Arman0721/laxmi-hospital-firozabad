Advanced Gastrology Diagnostics & Digestive Therapies

The Gastrology department at Laxmi Hospital Firozabad is a specialized clinic providing complete diagnosis and treatment profiles for digestive tract pathologies, covering the esophagus, stomach, small intestine, colon, liver, pancreas, and gallbladder. Our clinical team addresses chronic acid reflux, gastric ulcers, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), hepatitis, and liver cirrhosis.

We combine advanced video endoscopies and colonoscopies in dedicated diagnostic chambers to examine the mucosal linings directly, ensuring fast diagnosis and immediate relief mapping.

Gastrology FAQs

When should I consult a gastrologist for stomach pain?

You should consult a gastrologist if stomach pain is severe, persistent (lasting more than a few days), or accompanied by red-flag symptoms such as unexplained weight loss, recurring vomiting, difficulty swallowing, or blood in your stools.

How should I prepare for a diagnostic endoscopy?

Preparing for an upper GI endoscopy typically requires fasting (no food or liquids) for at least 6 to 8 hours before the procedure to ensure a completely clear view of your stomach. Our outpatient staff will provide you with custom instructions regarding medication timings prior to the test.
