CRITICAL ACCIDENT RESPONSE

Immediate Clinical Stabilization & Advanced Life Support Dispatch

Laxmi Hospital Firozabad is the designated critical care destination for highway trauma, cardiac blockages, cerebral strokes, severe burns, and pediatric crises. Our Emergency and Resuscitation ward operates continuously, providing experienced triage doctors and trauma teams without a single minute of delay.

Our Advanced Life Support (ALS) ambulances are fitted with in-transit ventilators, automated external defibrillators (AEDs), and telemetry modems to transmit vital data directly to our emergency desks during transit.

Core Clinical Treatments Offered

  • Emergency Triage System: Instant prioritization (Red, Yellow, Green zones) based on clinical severity to ensure zero stabilization delays.
  • Cardiac Defibrillation & Intubation: Advanced airway setups and immediate electrical cardioversion for cardiac arrest events.
  • Trauma Wound Controls: Hemostatic dressings, rapid vascular suturing, and in-transit blood transfusions for highway trauma cases.
  • Poison Triage Control: Advanced gastric lavage, charcoal absorptions, and specific clinical antidote infusions.
