Ear Nose Throat Clinic

Advanced Microscopic ENT Surgery & Sinusitis Therapies

The Ear, Nose, & Throat (ENT) department at Laxmi Hospital Firozabad is a specialized clinic providing complete diagnosis and treatment profiles for multiple auditory and respiratory pathways. Our team addresses chronic sinus conditions, tonsillar infections, voice hoarseness, deviated nasal septums (DNS), and hearing deficits.

We combine advanced video-endoscopies and clinical auditory screens to provide precise, comfortable diagnosis before performing microscopic ear or sinus surgeries.

Core Clinical Treatments Offered

  • Chronic Sinusitis Care: Advanced medication plans, nasal septum alignments, and functional endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS).
  • Microscopic Ear Surgery: Tympanoplasty for eardrum perforations and specialized therapies for chronic ear discharges.
  • Throat & Tonsillectomy: Complete pediatric tonsillectomy pathways and adenoid removals in highly hygienic settings.
  • Hearing Deficit Screening: Comprehensive audiometric diagnostic scheduling to identify early hearing loss.

ENT FAQs

When does a deviated nasal septum (DNS) require surgery?

A deviated nasal septum requires surgical correction (septoplasty) if it causes chronic nasal blockages, severe snoring, frequent sinus infections, or recurrent nosebleeds that fail to respond to standard medical nasal sprays.

Are pediatric tonsillectomies safe?

Yes. Pediatric tonsillectomies are routine and safe. Laxmi Hospital follows highly sterile pediatric guidelines and utilizes high-comfort techniques that ensure rapid post-operative healing and minimal throat discomfort for the child.
